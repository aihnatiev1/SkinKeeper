## Description (App Store — 4000 chars max)

Track your CS2 skin portfolio in real time. SkinKeeper is the only app that lets you manage your entire inventory, analyze profit & loss, sell on Steam Market, and trade between accounts — all from your phone.

PORTFOLIO DASHBOARD
See your total inventory value at a glance. Track 24-hour and 7-day changes. View profit & loss history with detailed charts. Know exactly how much you've made — or lost.

MULTI-SOURCE PRICING
Compare prices from Steam Market, Skinport, CSFloat, DMarket, and more. Find the best deal across marketplaces. Never sell below market value again.

SELL FROM YOUR PHONE
List items on Steam Market directly from the app. Quick sell at market price minus 1 cent, or set your own price. Bulk sell up to 50 items at once. Track listing progress in real time.

TRADE OFFERS
Send and accept trade offers without opening Steam. Browse your friends' inventories. Quick-transfer items between your own accounts with one tap.

PROFIT & LOSS TRACKING
Know your exact P&L per item, per account, and across your entire portfolio. Track cost basis automatically from Steam transaction history. Import manual purchases via CSV.

PRICE ALERTS
Set alerts when prices drop below your target or spike above average. Get push notifications instantly. Never miss a buying or selling opportunity.

MULTIPLE STEAM ACCOUNTS
Link all your Steam accounts. Switch between them instantly. See combined portfolio value or filter by account.

DETAILED ITEM INFO
Float values, paint seeds, stickers with placement, charms, Doppler phases, fade percentages. Everything you need to know about every skin.

HOME SCREEN WIDGET
Check your portfolio value without opening the app. See daily change at a glance.
